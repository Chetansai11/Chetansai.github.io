About Me

Passionate engineer building intelligent systems

Hi, I am a Master's student in Computer Engineering at Texas A&M University. I specialize in machine learning, computer vision, and robotics, with a passion for solving complex problems and creating innovative solutions. My experience spans designing intelligent control systems, building autonomous robots, and applying deep learning models to real-world challenges like image classification and healthcare analytics.

I enjoy blending theory with hands-on applications, constantly learning, and collaborating on impactful projects. My goal is to leverage technology to drive innovation and make a meaningful difference. Feel free to explore my work and connect!

When I'm not immersed in technology and problem-solving, I enjoy staying active through cricket and swimming, which help me maintain focus and balance. I'm passionate about exploring nature, connecting with people through sports, and constantly learning new skills outside my professional realm. These activities not only recharge me but also inspire creativity and teamwork in everything I do.

Interests
